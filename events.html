Events

September

Annual Monarch Celebration

Our celebration will take place the entire month of September to allow for social distancing!

Family friendly activities include:

  • Hike the Sugar Bush Trail around Cattail Pond and enjoy the self-guided Monarch trail, developed specially for the Monarch Celebration!
  • Follow our Facebook posts on Monarchs for cool facts and interesting info!
  • Fill out the Monarch Quiz Sheet

Through the sponsorship of the Troy Noon Optimists, we are able to continue celebrating Monarchs - a big shout out goes to all of them!

Cancelled due to COVID-19

Haunted Woods

Come enjoy a kid-friendly evening filled with costumed characters along a luminary-lit trail as you and your family learn all about the wild creatures of the night. Activities also include free face painting, crafts and games, wildlife viewing, storytelling at a campfire plus cookies and cider after the hike.

Cost:

  • $5 per person for BNC Members
  • $7 per person for non-members
  • Children ages 2 & under are free! cash or check only

Gates open at 6:00pm Trail times: 6:30-8:00pm A group of 20 people is led by a trail guide through the woods, with the last group leaving at 8:00pm. Estimated time on the trails is 45 minutes. Tickets are available on a first-come, first-served basis handed out in the order that you arrive at our entrance that evening. Parking is limited.

April 20th

5K Run Wild for Wildlife

If you enjoy running or hiking the woodland trails, you’ll love BNC’s VIRTUAL 5K Run Wild for Wildlife! For details about this event this year, click for the registration form. The deadline for registrations for participants wanting a super cool T-shirt is Wednesday, April 8 Registrations with no T-shirts must be postmarked by Thursday, April 16 or dropped off by Tuesday, April 21 at 7pm. You can run/hike anytime you want - like in the whole next year!! - but arrows for the course will be set up Saturday, April 25 – Sunday, May 10. Please don't all come at once - we must social distance, people! :) If the parking lot is packed, come back another time! Start training now!
