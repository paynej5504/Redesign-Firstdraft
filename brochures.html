Living with Wildlife

Thanks to the Kenneth A. Scott Charitable Trust, Brukner Nature Center has developed 13 species-specific brochures to help you live with the wildlife in your own backyard! Each brochure provides you with background information regarding the animal, ways to prevent problems from arising, recommended deterrents, laws protecting each wildlife, and other valuable information. All brochures are listed below. Simply click on the name and it will open a pdf file for you to review.
